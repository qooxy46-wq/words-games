QUIRKY GO for words📖

Learn Body Parts in English!

How?

Welcome to the QUIRKY's! There are 5 fun stages to help you learn 9 body parts words.

  • STAGE 1: Listen & Match - Listen to the word and choose the right picture
  • STAGE 2: Listen & Read - Listen to the word and choose the right spelling
  • STAGE 3: Word & Picture - Match words with their pictures
  • STAGE 4: Spelling Practice - Play 4 mini-games to practice spelling
  • STAGE 5: Dictation - Listen and write the word
